About

Capturing Rajasthan's living heritage

Based in the heart of Rajasthan, I'm a freelance photographer and videographer with a passion for people, portraits, and the vibrant life that surrounds us every day.

Every frame I shoot carries the warmth of desert light — the golden hour over ancient forts, the colour of festival crowds, the quiet dignity of a portrait taken in perfect light.

Whether it's a wedding, a creative concept, a personal portrait session, or an event, I bring the same intentionality to every project: tell a real story, beautifully.
